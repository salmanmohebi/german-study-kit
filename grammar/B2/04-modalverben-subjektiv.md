## Erklärung

Modal verbs have a **subjective use** — they express the speaker's certainty, claim, or hearsay rather than ability/permission/necessity.

| Modal | Subjektive Bedeutung |
|---|---|
| **müssen** | strong probability ("must be …") |
| **können** | possibility ("could be …") |
| **dürfte** (Konj. II) | likely ("is probably …") |
| **mag wohl** | possible/likely |
| **sollen** | hearsay — "supposedly" |
| **wollen** | a (often dubious) claim |

> *Er **muss** sehr klug sein.* — I'm pretty sure he is. (not "he must be smart" as a duty)
> *Er **soll** reich sein.* — They say he's rich. (hearsay)
> *Er **will** Arzt sein.* — He claims to be a doctor.

For past assumptions, add infinitive in past form: *Er muss verschlafen haben.*

## Sätze

- Er {{c1::muss}} sehr klug sein, denn er hat alles richtig gemacht. | He must be very smart — he got everything right.
- Sie {{c1::kann}} nicht zu Hause sein, das Licht ist aus. | She can't be home, the lights are off.
- Er {{c1::soll}} sehr reich sein. | He's supposedly very rich.
- Du {{c1::dürftest}} müde sein nach der langen Reise. | You must be tired after the long trip.
- Er {{c1::will}} ein berühmter Arzt sein. | He claims to be a famous doctor.
- Sie {{c1::muss}} schon zu Hause sein, ihr Auto steht da. | She must be home — her car is there.
- Das {{c1::kann}} nicht wahr sein! | That can't be true!
- Er {{c1::soll}} viele Sprachen sprechen. | He's said to speak many languages.
- Das {{c1::dürfte}} kein Problem sein. | That probably isn't a problem.
- Sie {{c1::will}} alles gewusst haben. | She claims to have known everything.
- Er {{c1::muss}} verschlafen haben. | He must have overslept.
- Sie {{c1::könnte}} es vergessen haben. | She might have forgotten it.
- Es {{c1::soll}} morgen regnen. | They say it will rain tomorrow.
- Er {{c1::muss}} schon angekommen sein. | He must have already arrived.
- Sie {{c1::dürfte}} bald kommen. | She's likely to come soon.
- Das {{c1::kann}} nicht stimmen. | That can't be right.
- Er {{c1::will}} den Schlüssel verloren haben. | He claims to have lost the key.
- Sie {{c1::soll}} sehr nett sein. | She's said to be very nice.
- Das {{c1::muss}} ein Missverständnis gewesen sein. | That must have been a misunderstanding.
- Er {{c1::dürfte}} das nicht wissen. | He probably doesn't know that.
