## Erklärung

These conjunctions all push the **conjugated verb to the end** of the subclause:

| Konj. | Bedeutung |
|---|---|
| **obwohl** | although |
| **damit** | so that (purpose) |
| **falls** | in case |
| **bevor** | before |
| **nachdem** | after (often + Plusquamperfekt) |
| **während** | while / whereas |
| **sobald** | as soon as |
| **solange** | as long as |
| **sodass / so dass** | so that (result) |
| **da** | since (formal "because") |

When the subclause comes first, the next clause starts with V1: *Obwohl es regnet, **gehe** ich spazieren.*

## Sätze

- {{c1::Obwohl}} es regnet, gehe ich spazieren. | Although it's raining, I'm going for a walk.
- Ich lerne fleißig, {{c1::damit}} ich die Prüfung bestehe. | I'm studying hard so that I pass the exam.
- {{c1::Falls}} du Hilfe brauchst, ruf mich an. | In case you need help, call me.
- {{c1::Bevor}} ich schlafe, lese ich noch. | Before I sleep, I read a bit more.
- {{c1::Nachdem}} ich gegessen hatte, ging ich joggen. | After I had eaten, I went jogging.
- {{c1::Während}} ich kochte, hörte ich Musik. | While I was cooking, I listened to music.
- {{c1::Sobald}} der Bus kommt, steigen wir ein. | As soon as the bus comes, we get on.
- {{c1::Solange}} du hier bist, kannst du bleiben. | As long as you're here, you can stay.
- Es regnete stark, {{c1::sodass}} wir zu Hause blieben. | It rained heavily, so that we stayed home.
- {{c1::Da}} ich krank bin, kann ich nicht kommen. | Since I'm sick, I can't come.
- {{c1::Obwohl}} sie müde ist, arbeitet sie weiter. | Although she's tired, she keeps working.
- Wir sparen, {{c1::damit}} wir ein Haus kaufen können. | We're saving so that we can buy a house.
- {{c1::Bevor}} du gehst, mach bitte das Licht aus. | Before you leave, please turn off the light.
- {{c1::Nachdem}} der Film zu Ende war, gingen wir nach Hause. | After the film was over, we went home.
- {{c1::Während}} er telefonierte, schrieb ich eine E-Mail. | While he was on the phone, I wrote an email.
- {{c1::Sobald}} ich Zeit habe, rufe ich dich an. | As soon as I have time, I'll call you.
- {{c1::Solange}} es so kalt ist, gehe ich nicht raus. | As long as it's this cold, I'm not going out.
- Sie übte viel, {{c1::sodass}} sie die Prüfung bestand. | She practiced a lot, so that she passed the exam.
- {{c1::Falls}} es regnet, nimm einen Schirm. | In case it rains, take an umbrella.
- {{c1::Obwohl}} er reich ist, lebt er bescheiden. | Although he's rich, he lives modestly.
