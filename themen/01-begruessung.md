## Erklärung

Greetings and small-talk phrases for meeting people, starting and ending conversations. The right greeting depends on time of day and how well you know the person.

## Sätze

- Guten Morgen! | Good morning!
- Guten Abend! | Good evening!
- Gute Nacht! | Good night!
- Wie geht's? | How's it going? (informal)
- Mir geht's super. | I'm great.
- Nicht so gut. | Not so good.
- Schön, Sie kennenzulernen. | Nice to meet you.
- Freut mich. | Pleased to meet you.
- Bis morgen! | See you tomorrow!
- Bis bald! | See you soon!
- Schönes Wochenende! | Have a nice weekend!
- Schönen Tag noch! | Have a nice day!
- Was machst du heute Abend? | What are you doing tonight?
- Schön, dich wiederzusehen. | Nice to see you again.
- Lange nicht gesehen! | Long time no see!
