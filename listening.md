# Listening curriculum

Goal: 20-30 min/day passive + active listening. Move up a tier when the current one feels easy.

## A1 → A2

- **Coffee Break German** (Radio Lingua) — structured, English-explained, ideal for A1.
- **GermanPod101** — Absolute Beginner / Beginner series.

## A2 → B1

- **Slow German mit Annik Rubens** — slow, clear, monologue-style. Free transcripts.
- **Easy German Podcast** — natural conversational German, transcripts on Patreon.
- **Nachrichtenleicht** (Deutschlandfunk) — weekly news in plain German.

## B1 → B2

- **DW Langsam gesprochene Nachrichten** — daily news, slow but native register.
- **Easy German Podcast** (full speed).
- **Auf Deutsch gesagt!** — long-form interviews, B1-B2.

## B2+

- **Tagesschau in 100 Sekunden** — native-speed news.
- **Lage der Nation** — politics podcast, native register.

## Active listening drill

Once a week, pick one 5-minute clip and:
1. Listen once without transcript.
2. Listen with transcript, look up unknowns.
3. Mine 3-5 sentences into `sentences/<source>.md` for next deck rebuild.
